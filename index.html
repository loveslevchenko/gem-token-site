About the Project

GEM Token is a community-driven ERC-20 token built to support secure, transparent, and decentralized digital assets. Our goal is to empower holders and build a strong Web3 ecosystem around GEM.

The project is led by a small, decentralized team of crypto enthusiasts, developers, and designers who believe in open finance and transparency. We’re committed to long-term development and community growth.
